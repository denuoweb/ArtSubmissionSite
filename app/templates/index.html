Quest by Cycle's Badge Contest

Quest by Cycle is an innovative game that encourages cycling, sustainability, and community engagement. Players earn points by completing various quests, and digital badges are awarded as a form of recognition and achievement.

This project is funded by a grant from the Oregon Cultural Trust. Support the Trust to sustain arts, heritage, and humanities projects by making a matching donation and receiving the match back as a tax credit. Learn more at culturaltrust.org.

We are calling for artists to help us design these unique badges, bringing their creativity and vision to life. The badges will not only represent different quests but also inspire players to continue their journey toward sustainability.
