Quest by Cycle's Badge Contest

Quest by Cycle is an innovative game that encourages cycling, sustainability, and community engagement. Players earn points by completing various quests, and digital badges are awarded as a form of recognition and achievement.

We are calling for artists to help us design these unique badges, bringing their creativity and vision to life. The badges will not only represent different quests but also inspire players to continue their journey toward sustainability.

Award notification will be made between April 1 - 15, 2025.

Two calls for artists are open:

  • General Call for Artists: This is open to all Lane County artists. Ten winning artists will be awarded $200 each.
  • Youth Artists (13 - 18 years old): This is open to young Lane County artists to contribute their artwork and win exciting prizes. Seven youth artists will be awarded prizes such as bike lights, pumps, fenders, and other accessories.

Questions or Feedback? We are committed to transparency and welcome all inquiries. Contact us at info@QuestByCycle.org. To check out the game, please visit our website at QuestByCycle.org.
