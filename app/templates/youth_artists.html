Call for Youth Artists

Are you creative and love to draw? Do you care about the environment and want to make a difference? We’re looking for young artists (18 and under) to help design cool badges for Quest by Cycle (QbC), a game where people earn points for biking and helping the planet!

Eligibility

Anyone aged 18 and younger can apply. No experience is needed—just creativity and excitement!

What You’ll Do

  • Create fun, colorful designs for badges that people will win in the game.
  • Make digital drawings or hand-drawn sketches that will be turned into digital badges.

How to Apply

  • Show Us Your Work: Send 1-2 drawings (either a photo of your hand-drawn art or a digital file).
  • Tell Us About You: Write a few sentences telling us who you are and why you love to draw.
  • Contact Information: Include your name, age, and a parent/guardian’s email or phone number (so we can reach out).

Email your submissions to [email address] with the subject line: "Young Artist Badge Submission – [Your Name]".

Prizes and Recognition

  • Your design featured in the Quest by Cycle game!
  • A prize valued at $30 (e.g., bike accessories, gift certificates).

Deadline

Submissions must be received by [Insert Deadline].

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at [contact email]. We can’t wait to see your amazing art!
