Call for Youth Artists

Are you creative and love to draw? Do you care about the environment and want to make a difference? We’re looking for young artists (18 and under) to help design cool badges for Quest by Cycle, a game where people earn points for biking and helping the planet!

Prizes and Recognition

  • Your design featured in the Quest by Cycle game!
  • A prize valued at ~$30 (e.g., bike accessories, etc).

Eligibility

Anyone between 13 and 18 can apply. No experience is needed—just creativity and excitement!

What You’ll Do

  • Create fun, colorful designs for badges that people will win in the game.
  • Make digital drawings or hand-drawn sketches that will be turned into digital badges.

How to Apply
